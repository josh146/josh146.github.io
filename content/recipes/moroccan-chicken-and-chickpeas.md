Title: Roast chicken with chickpeas
Slug: roast-chicken-with-chickpeas
Date: 2026-05-23
Category: Roasts
Time: 45 minutes
Servings: 4
Image: /images/recipes/moroccan-chicken-chickpeas.jpg

## Ingredients

* 6 bone-in chicken thighs
* Lemon slices
* Coriander and/or dill

### Marinade

* 1 tbsp baharat spice mix[ref]You can substitute the baharat spice mix with harissa paste[/ref]
* 1 tsp ground cumin 
* 1 tsp smoked paprika
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tsp black pepper
* 2 garlic cloves (minced)
* 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
* 3 tbsp olive oil

### Chickpeas and tomatoes

* 800g chickpeas (drained)
* 225g cherry tomatoes
* 1 tbsp olive oil
* 1 tsp turmeric
* 1 tsp ground ginger
* 1/2 tsp cumin
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp pepper

## Steps

1. In a large bowl, mix the marinade ingredients and add the chicken --- toss well to coat. Place in
the fridge, and marinate for at least 4 hours and up to 24.

1. Preheat the oven to 220ºC.

1. Combine the chickpeas and tomatoes in a bowl with the olive oil, turmeric, ground ginger, cumin,
salt, and pepper.

1. On a baking paper-lined baking tray, place the marinated chicken thighs[ref]You could also approach this dish similar to [[One-pot chicken and rice]]. After marinading, sear the chicken skin-side down in a pan, and remove. Fry onions, garlic, ginger, turmeric, harissa, and cumin. Add the chickpeas, tomatoes, and some stock or water. Return the chicken, and bake in the oven for 20 minutes[/ref]. Add the chickpeas and
tomato mixture, filling up the gaps between the chicken. Arrange the lemon slices amongst the
chickpeas. Bake for 35-40 minutes, until the chicken is browned and crispy.

1. Serve garnished with coriander and/or dill.
