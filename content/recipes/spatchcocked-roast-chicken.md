An easy roast chicken recipe, this is a convenient weeknight meal --- simply prepare the marinade
and chicken the night before, and leave in the fridge ready to roast.

<div id="variation-controls"></div>

## Ingredients

* 1 whole chicken (approximately 1.5-1.8kg)

{{additional_list}}

### Marinade

A simple dry marinade is simply salt and pepper. For dry brining, aim for approximately 1% salt.

{{marinade_list}}

### Aromatic base

You can place the chicken directly in a baking tray on baking paper, or if you would like you can place the chicken on top of an aromatic base of vegetables and herbs.

## Steps

1. Spatchcock the chicken. Cut along both sides of the backbone (save the backbone for [[chicken stock]]), flip the chicken over, and push down on the breastbone to flatten the chicken.
Place in a baking tray on baking paper, on top of an aromatic base (if using).

{{marinade_step}}

1.  Marinate and place in the fridge for a minimum of 1 hour, but preferably overnight[ref]If the marinade is acidic --- e.g., it contains lemon juice or yogurt, try to avoid marinating for more than 5 hours.[/ref], to marinate and brine.

{{additional_step}}

1. Preheat the oven to 200ºC. Roast the chicken for 55-60 minutes, rest for 10-15 minutes, and then carve and serve.
